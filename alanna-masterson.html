Professional Life

Professionally, Alanna Masterson is an American Actress. In 1994, she made her acting debut through the television show  The Young and Restless in the role of Colleen Carlton at the age of six. Later she promoted to a series regular for the fifth season and continued to the main credits in the seventh season. Further, from 2014 to 2019, she appeared as herself in the show Talking Dead. In 2018, she played the role of Kiara Johnson in Younger, and also appeared in the film Afraid as Alanna Meadows.
