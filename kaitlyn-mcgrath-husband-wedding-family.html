Kaitlyn McGrath Wiki: Age, Family & Education

Kaitlyn McGrath recently turned to the age of 29 in early 2021. She was born in the year 1992 and celebrates her birthday on the 2nd of January every year. Her birth sign is Capricorn. She was born on her grandmother’s 62nd birthday.


She is the daughter of parents Kathy McGrath and Paul McGrath. Moreover, she has an elder brother named Ryan McGrath. She grew up with her brother and family in Pittsburgh, a city in western Pennsylvania.

A passionate dancer, she started dancing at the age of three. After completing high school, she went to the University of Florida. She earned her bachelor’s degree in Telecommunications, News Broadcasting in 2014.

Moreover, she received a Certificate of Geosciences- Broadcast and Operational Meteorology from Mississippi State University in 2017.

Joined NBC Connecticut In 2015

Kaitlyn interned at CBS-owned and operated KDKA-TV, where she contributed to its show, Pittsburgh Today Live. Moreover, she was a student reporter at the university-owned WUFT in Florida and worked as a weather forecasting anchor.

McGrath began her journalism career in 2014 as a Weather Forecaster at a CBS-affiliated WCSC-TV. There, she served as a weekday evening and weekend morning weather anchor.


Moreover, she produced feature stories for numerous topics and appeared on live newscasts. She worked at the station for a year until 2015.

In 2015, she joined NBC Connecticut and started to work as a Meteorologist. As of now, she delivers the weather news from various parts of Connecticut.
