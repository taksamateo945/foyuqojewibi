Snake Big Enough to Swallow a Human Sends TikTok Users Into Frenzy: Man Sets Trap to Catch the Huge

Snakes are sneaky critters and one of the most feared things in the world. Brave men set a trap to catch a snake that looked big enough to swallow a human whole.

Most people have watched terrifying movies where snakes eat humans, and it has left people fearful for life. No matter how big or small the snake, best believe someone will be screaming.

TikTok user @joshchancey55 shared a video showing how the men caught the huge snake. It is an eye-opener watching the snake come out of the water – it is ginormous!

Getting the professionals take on the giant snake

Briefly News spoke to Ralph Riggens from Ultimate Exotics to find out if this snake could actually eat a human. Turns out, it can! Here is what he said:

"The snake in the video is a reticulated python, they are known to be the longest snake in the world. "They have been known to prey on humans and although the snake in the video doesn't look like it's big enough to eat an adult, it is definitely a threat to children."
