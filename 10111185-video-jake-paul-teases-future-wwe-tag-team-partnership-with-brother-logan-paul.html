Video: Jake Paul Teases Future WWE Tag Team Partnership with Brother Logan Paul | News, Scores, High

Publish date: 2024-09-06
Alex Menendez/Getty Images

The Paul brothers have been forging their own paths in combat sports, but it sounds like there's a possibility of them working as a team in the future.

During his pre-fight press conference for Saturday's matchup against Ryan Bourland, Jake Paul was asked about the chances of facing Logan Paul in a boxing match or a WWE match, and he responded that he'd rather join forces with his brother.

"I see a world where we're WWE tag team partners, that would be sick," Jake Paul said. "Honestly, like, who would stop us?"

Logan Paul currently holds the WWE United States Championship. He participated in the men's Elimination Chamber match last Saturday at the premium live event in Perth, Australia.

While Jake Paul has yet to test his hand in a WWE ring, his commitment to boxing has resulted in an 8-1 record with five wins by knockout. He will be seeking his third straight victory when he meets Bourland in San Juan, Puerto Rico.
