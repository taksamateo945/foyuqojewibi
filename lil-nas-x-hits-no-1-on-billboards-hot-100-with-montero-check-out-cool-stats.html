Lil Nas X Hits No. 1 on Billboard's Hot 100 with 'Montero' - Check Out Cool Stats!

Publish date: 2024-06-10

Lil Nas X has earned his second number one song on the Billboard Hot 100 chart with his new song “Montero (Call Me By Your Name).”

The 21-year-old rapper previously broke the record for the most weeks spent at number one with his debut single “Old Town Road,” which ruled the chart for 19 weeks in 2019.

The success of “Montero” is fueled by the song’s buzzworthy music video, which was released on March 26.

Click inside to see cool stats about the song’s debut on the chart…

The song is the 51st single to debut at number one on the chart and it’s the 1,120th number one song in the history of the chart.

“Montero” debuted at number one on the Streaming Songs chart with 46.9 million U.S. streams and at number two on the Digital Song Sales chart with 21,000 downloads.

If you didn’t know, Montero is Lil Nas X‘s real first name and this marks the second song in less than a year to shout-out the name of the artist and also top the chart. Travis Scott and Kid Cudi‘s song “The Scotts” topped the chart last year!
