How Tall is J. I. Prince?

The American rapper has a height of 5 feet 8 inches which is the equivalent of 172 cm and 1.72 meters. According to various reports, the average height for men in America is 5 feet 9.3 inches; that is, 176 centimeters and 1.76 meters. This means that most men in the United States are slightly taller than J.I Prince of New York.

However, the rapper is taller than most men from other countries, especially in South Asia, where the average height for men is 5 feet 5 inches; that is 165 centimeters and 1.65 meters. What is J.I. Prince’s Age?

J.I Prince of New York is 22 years old at the moment. He was born on the 23rd of September 2001, and his birth sign is Libra. According to several reports, he was born in Brooklyn, New York City, where he was also raised.

The rapper was introduced to music at a young age by his parents. It is said that they played old-school hip hop and reggaeton which influenced him to start making simple beats. By the time he was in middle school, at the age of 11, he was already freestyling with his brothers. Shortly after, he started sharing his freestyle on Instagram, which caught the attention of Jermaine Dupri.

He Gained Mainstream Popularity in 2016 at the Age of 15

J.I the Prince of N.Y was 15 years old when Jermaine Dupri discovered him on Instagram. The producer had him participate in the second season of his reality show competition, The Rap Game. He performed well in the competition, and it gained him mainstream popularity. All of these happened in 2016.

The following year when he was 16, he released his first project, a three-track extended play called Barely Famous. The years that followed saw him give up on pursuing his music career. Things were so rough he was kicked out of his mother’s house. Desperate to find a way to survive, he resumed making music and then was signed to Interscope Records in 2019 at the age of 18.
