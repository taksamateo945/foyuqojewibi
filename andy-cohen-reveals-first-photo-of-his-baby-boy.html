Andy Cohen Reveals First Photo Of His Baby Boy!

Publish date: 2024-07-06

The New Year Eve 2018 was something special for American TV personality Andy Cohen, as he revealed the gender of his baby on that day. Now the suspense is disclosed; Andy reveals the face of his newly born baby boy.

Andy Cohen, the Emmy-winning reality television producer and his and his newly-born son, Benjamin Allen Cohen appear on the cover of People magazine on February 2019. The baby-father duo appears staring at the camera.

Previously, The Bravo host announced the birth of his surrogate baby boy through his Instagram post on the 4th of February 2019.

“WOW! This is my son, Benjamin Allen Cohen. He is 9 lbs 2 ounces !!” “20 inches !! Born at 6:35 pm, PT He is named after my grandfather Ben Allen. I’m in love. And speechless. And eternally grateful to an incredible surrogate. And I’m a dad. Wow.” Andy wrote on his Instagram.

Though Cohen shared several pictures with his son on social media, the baby’s face remained secret. Now, the babyface features on the cover of People magazine.

Andy poses with his newly born surrogate son Benjamin Allen Cohen for the cover of People magazine in February 2019. (Picture Source: Eonline )

In the cover story of the magazine, Cohen opens up about the birth of baby Benjamin. He also talks about the organization that helped him find the egg donor and the surrogate, and kind of package the whole process. He even thanks the incredible surrogate to have worked with.

Andy revealed that he is going to be dad soon during an episode of the late-night talk show Watch What Happens Live on 20th December 2018.

The openly gay TV queer man is also known for his engrossing dating life. He previously dated Ivy league graduate Clifton Dassuncao. The duo starting dating since 2016, but their split was confirmed as Andy stated that he is single to Entertainment Tonight in March 2018. The unmarried Andy was linked with American singer John Mayer, however, he denied the rumors in a CNN interview in 2018.

Andy Cohen Career, Net Worth

Andy, who started his career from the CBS News, summons the net worth of $15 million dollars. The New York Times best-selling author is the executive producer and host of the Bravo’s late night, interactive talk show, Watch What Happens Live with Andy Cohen.

Andy Cohen served as the Bravo’s Executive Vice President of Development and Talent till 2013. (Picture Source: Aol)

Andy, aged 50 is an executive producer of the Real Housewives franchise. Besides, he hosts Andy Cohen Live on SiriusXM channel 102, and the television dating show, Love Connection.

Andy Cohen Bio: Parents, Education, Height

The multi-talented personality was born as Andrew Joseph Cohen on 2nd June 1968 to parents Evelyn and Lou Cohen. Growing up in St. Louis, Missouri, he attended Clayton High School, graduating in 1986.

Andy, who stands at the height of 5 feet 9 inches (1.75 m) received a Bachelor of Science degree in broadcast journalism from the Boston University.
