Khalyla Kuhn Met Bobby Lee On Tinder

Dating sites like Tinder are for finding love or a date and Khalyla Kuhn and Bobby Lee made use of Tinder for the exact purpose it was created. The lovebirds met on the dating app in 2013 and after chatting for a while, they decided to meet up for a coffee date.

They made plans and arrangements for their first date and finally met at a coffee shop in Long Beach, California. After the meeting, Khalyla and Bobby didn’t stop seeing each other, as they moved from Tinder to begin an actual relationship.

They Started TigerBelly Podcast Together

In 2016, Khalyla Kuhn and Bobby Lee started a podcast called TigerBelly. Their podcast is available on YouTube and iTunes. In no time, their podcast videos started amassing a large number of views and today they have over 800k subscribers on YouTube.

On their podcast, they talk freely about things like politics, sexuality, racism, and Asian-American related topics. They refer to their podcast show as the #1 Asian podcast of all time. Sometimes they invite guests to discuss with and other times it’s just the two of them.

Meanwhile, Khalyla Kuhn also co-hosts another podcast dubbed ‘Trash Tuesday” with her friends Annie Lederman and Esther Povitsky.

Their Relationship Began To Go South When Khalyla Kuhn Fell Sick

Things began to go south for the TigerBelly podcasters some years ago when Khalyla Kuhn experienced some health issues.

During this period, Bobby Lee had to look after her, but, unfortunately, taking care of her made him begin to see her from a different perspective. According to him, being her caregiver when she was sick made it difficult for him to see her on a romantic note.

Khalyla and Bobby tried what they could to salvage their relationship situation, however, from the look of things, being together for so long made them desire other people romantically. And so on an episode of their podcast, they joking said they would like to be with other people.

Well, it did look like a mere joke because Bobby suggested that Khalyla gets someone else because he no longer had any romantic feelings for her, but was still in love with her.

Bobby Schaub Accused Khalyla Kuhn Of Wanting to Have an Affair With Him

During one of the episodes of Trash Tuesday, Khalyla and her friends were talking about a particular comedian who has made sexual advances at them. That episode of Trash Tuesday became so popular that the comedian was discovered to be Bobby Schaub. Fans talked about it to the point that Bobby Schaub accused TigerBelly hosts of defaming his image.

As a result of the heat, Bobby Schaub was invited to TigerBelly for a podcast discussion with Bobby Lee and Khalyla Kuhn, however, they couldn’t come to any resolution. Rather Bobby Schaub revealed that he had evidence to prove that Khalyla Kuhn had interest in having an affair with him.

Khalyla has made it clear that she hates Bobby Schaub’s guts and is not afraid of whatever evidence he claims to have. Schaub is yet to provide the evidence he boasted about.

Khalyla and Bobby Announced Their Breakup On TigerBelly

Khalyla Kuhn and Bobby Lee could not find a way to settle the tension that was building up in their relationship. As a result, they decided it was best to go their separate ways. The news about their split was announced during one of the TigerBelly episodes. It was a difficult news for Bobby Lee to announce and during the announcement, Khalyla Kuhn could be seen wiping tears from her eyes.

Their separation was not only difficult for the former love birds, but for their fans as well. People had gotten so used to them that they never wanted to see them part ways. Well, it happened and sadly, the issue between Bobby Schaub and Khalyla Kuhn was one of the things that finally broke the Carmel’s back.

They are still co-hosting TigerBelly as friends and neither of them have gone into another relationship.
